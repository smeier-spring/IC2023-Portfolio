You ignore the strange singing and continue walking through the forest. After awhile you feel a sharp pain in your back. An elf shot you with a poisoned arrow, and you collapse. You awaken bound to a pole, hanging over a fire. You are assaulted by the smell of your own roasting flesh.
The singing from earlier and dancing elves surround you.

Eventually You die.

Incidently, the elves had a wonderful feast that night.
